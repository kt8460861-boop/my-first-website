Manchester city 소개

Manchester City is an English professional football club based in Manchester, widely known as "The Citizens." \ They compete in the Premier League and have become one of the most dominant and successful clubs in Europe since the 2010s. The club has won numerous domestic trophies, including multiple Premier League titles and their first UEFA Champions League in 2023.

Their home ground is the Etihad Stadium, which they moved to in 2003. Since the takeover in 2008 by the Abu Dhabi United Group, the club has experienced immense financial investment and global growth. Under manager Pep Guardiola, they have implemented a unique, possession-based, and high-pressing style of play, achieving historic treble wins. City's main rivalry is with neighboring club Manchester United, known as the Manchester Derby.
