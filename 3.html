Manchester United 소개

Manchester United is one of the world's most famous and historic football clubs, nicknamed "The Red Devils." They hold the record for the most English league titles, dominating the Premier League era. Their success includes numerous domestic cups and three UEFA Champions League titles, cementing their global reputation.

Their iconic home stadium is Old Trafford, known as "The Theatre of Dreams." The club's greatest era was under legendary manager Sir Alex Ferguson (1986–2013), who secured an unprecedented number of trophies. Manchester United is one of the wealthiest clubs globally, supported by a massive worldwide fanbase. Their main and fierce local derby is against their neighbor, Manchester City.
